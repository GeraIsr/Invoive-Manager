FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Invoice Manager before you download.

Is Invoice Manager free to download? ▾
Yes — Invoice Manager is completely free to download on Google Play. Core features including invoice creation, client management, and time tracking are available at no cost. A Pro subscription with advanced features is coming soon.
Is my financial data secure? ▾
Absolutely. All data is stored in Firebase Firestore with strict security rules. Your account is protected by Firebase Authentication. We never share your data with third parties. You can also enable biometric lock within the app settings.
Which currencies does the app support? ▾
Invoice Manager supports 10+ currencies including EUR, USD, GBP, RUB, KZT, UAH, CHF, PLN, SEK, and more. You can set a default currency per client, so invoicing international clients is seamless. Live exchange rate integration is coming in a future update.
Can I customize the PDF invoices with my brand? ▾
Yes! You can upload your logo, choose brand colors, customize fonts, and add a personalized footer message to every PDF invoice. The result is a professional, branded document that represents your business perfectly.
Is the app available in Russian? ▾
Yes — Invoice Manager is fully localized in both English and Russian. The app automatically detects your device language. Contract templates are also provided in both languages, specifically designed for Russian legal requirements and CIS freelancers.
Does the time tracker integrate with invoices? ▾
Yes — this is one of our most loved features. When you create an invoice for a project, you can pull all tracked time sessions directly into invoice line items. The app automatically calculates the total based on your hourly rate. No manual entry needed.
When is the iOS / web version coming? ▾
We're actively working on the iOS version and a web dashboard. Since the app is built with Flutter, the iOS port benefits from our shared codebase and is targeted for later in 2026. Sign up for notifications to be first to know when it launches.
